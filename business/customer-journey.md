# Customer Journey Map
## Overview
The customer journey map for api-sentry outlines the phases a potential customer goes through from initial awareness to expansion of the service. This map highlights trigger events, friction points, user emotions, opportunities to delight, and key metrics for each phase.

## Aware
* **Trigger Event**: Encounter with LLM API performance degradation, leading to user complaints or internal monitoring alerts.
* **Friction Points**: Lack of visibility into API performance issues, difficulty in identifying the root cause of problems.
* **User Emotions**: Frustration, concern about user experience and potential loss of business.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Introduction to api-sentry as a proactive solution for detecting and notifying about API performance degradation.
* **Metric**: Number of unique visitors to the api-sentry website or social media channels.

## Consider
* **Trigger Event**: Research into solutions for LLM API performance monitoring, potentially discovering api-sentry through search engines, developer forums, or word-of-mouth.
* **Friction Points**: Evaluating the effectiveness and cost of api-sentry compared to existing solutions or DIY approaches.
* **User Emotions**: Curiosity, skepticism about the value proposition of api-sentry.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Offering a free trial or demo, showcasing success stories, and highlighting the unique selling points of api-sentry.
* **Metric**: Number of sign-ups for the free trial or requests for demos.

## Try
* **Trigger Event**: Signing up for a free trial of api-sentry to test its features and effectiveness.
* **Friction Points**: Difficulty in setting up the service, interpreting alerts, or integrating with existing workflows.
* **User Emotions**: Hope for a solution, apprehension about potential complexity or false positives.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Providing easy-to-follow setup guides, responsive customer support, and clear documentation.
* **Metric**: Trial conversion rate to paid plans, measured by the number of users who upgrade from the free trial to a paid subscription.

## Adopt
* **Trigger Event**: Decision to subscribe to a paid plan of api-sentry after a successful trial period.
* **Friction Points**: Ongoing costs, potential for alert fatigue, or integration challenges with other tools.
* **User Emotions**: Relief at having a reliable solution, anticipation of improved API performance and user satisfaction.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular updates with new features, excellent customer support, and proactive outreach to ensure satisfaction.
* **Metric**: Customer retention rate, measured by the percentage of subscribers who remain active over a specified period.

## Expand
* **Trigger Event**: Realization of the value provided by api-sentry, leading to consideration of additional features or services.
* **Friction Points**: Concerns about increasing costs, evaluating the need for more advanced features.
* **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, potential eagerness to explore more comprehensive solutions.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Offering tiered plans with additional features, providing personalized recommendations for service optimization.
* **Metric**: Average revenue per user (ARPU) growth, indicating the success of upselling and cross-selling strategies.

By understanding and addressing the needs, emotions, and pain points at each phase of the customer journey, api-sentry can improve user satisfaction, increase conversion rates, and ultimately drive business growth.
